We use 2 systems during step 1 of the 5 steps, our Introduction. 


--------------------------------
SEE – CREATE / GET ENGAGEMENT
--------------------------------
Principle:
Engagement is a two-way exchange. You must give it to get it.

Components:
- Smile
  - Makes you approachable
  - Shows positivity
- Eye Contact
  - Shows engagement
  - Builds confidence and trust
- Enthusiasm
  - Energy transfer

Reminder:
- Not everyone will match your energy
- Neutral energy people can still be great donors
- SEE should also be used throughout the entire pitch. 



--------------------------------
ISH – STOPPING PEOPLE
--------------------------------
Interest (~20 ft):
- Goal: Grab attention
- How: “Hi! How are you?”

Slow / Stop (~15 ft):
- Goal: Slow or stop their pace
- How: “Before you go in…”

Hook (~10 ft):
- Goal: Get them to the table
- How: “Come check us out!”

Pro Tip:
- Asking questions gives people an easy exit
- Use a big arm hook while turning away and breaking eye contact
- If they don’t have a face to say no to, they often follow
