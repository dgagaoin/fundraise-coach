# Code of Development — First Week (FR)

> **Purpose:** This document outlines the required knowledge, skills, and checkpoints for a fundraiser’s **first week**.
> It is used by leaders to ensure consistency, confidence, and readiness before full independence.

Each section is tracked as:

* **Learned** — Taught, explained, and understood
* **Tested** — Demonstrated in the field or through roleplay

---

## Intro Into the Business

**Required Topics**

* Company background
* Product / charity training
* ROI / MSF
* Pay structure & new start bonuses
* Quality standards
* Compliance
* ADP
* Introduction to the 5 Steps

**Goal:** Build context, trust, and clarity around *why* we do what we do.

---

## Collateral

**Required Topics**

* Pitch cards
* Product / charity collateral

**Goal:** Ensure the fundraiser can confidently reference and use approved materials.

---

## Basics

**Required Topics**

* 3 Types of Day
* S.E.E
* KISS
* Law of Averages

**Goal:** Establish foundational mental models for consistency and resilience.

---

## 5 Steps to a Sale

**Required Steps**

1. Introduction / ISH
2. Presentation / Rapport
3. Short Story / TOV & BL
4. Close / GIFTS
5. Rehash / iPad

**Goal:** Create a clear, repeatable sales flow that can be coached and refined.

---

## Selling Yourself

**Framework**

* 4 Steps to selling yourself

**Goal:** Help the fundraiser understand that *they* are part of the value exchange.

## KPIs (Performance Awareness)

**Tracked Concepts**

* iPad
* S.E.E

* Troubleshooting

**Goal:** Build early awareness of controllables, feedback loops, and performance indicators.
