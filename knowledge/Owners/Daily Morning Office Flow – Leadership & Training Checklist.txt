# Daily Morning Office Flow – Leadership & Training Checklist

> **Use this checklist to ensure the full morning experience is delivered consistently, intentionally, and at a high standard.**

This mirrors how the office actually runs — and how culture, performance, and motivation are built daily.

---

## ⬜ 8:00 AM — Leaders Meeting (Owners / Senior Leaders)

**Purpose:** Set the tone, reinforce standards, develop leaders

* Delivered by an owner or senior leader
* Focus areas:

  * Leadership principles
  * Accountability & ownership
  * Standards & expectations
  * People development
* Pulls from:

  * Leadership RAG
  * Leaders Meetings library

> *This meeting sharpens the leaders before they lead others.*

---

## ⬜ 8:30 AM — Crew Meetings (5-Minute Huddles)

**Purpose:** Motivate, align, and emotionally connect each team

* Separate huddles per crew/team
* 3–5 minutes each
* Focus areas:

  * Energy
  * Belief
  * Purpose
  * Short, punchy motivation
* Pulls from:

  * Motivational Stories RAG
  * 2-min / 5-min / 10-min motivational formats

> *This is where belief is transferred person-to-person.*

---

## ⬜ 8:35–9:15 AM — Technical Sales Training

**Purpose:** Build skill, confidence, and consistency

* 30–45 minutes total
* Core components:

  * The pitch
  * 5 steps
  * Rebuttals
  * AIC
  * Practice drills
  * Systems & process knowledge
* Emphasis on:

  * Repetition
  * Skill development
  * Feedback
* Pulls from:

  * Sales Techniques Library
  * Systems Knowledge RAG

> *Confidence comes from competence.*

---

## ⬜ 9:15–9:30 AM — Sales Impact (Whole Office)

**Purpose:** Elevate sales thinking and real-world application

* Delivered by an owner or senior leader
* 10-minute segment
* Focus areas:

  * One sales concept
  * One insight
  * One takeaway
* Pulls from:

  * Sales Concepts Library

> *Short, sharp, and actionable.*

---

## ⬜ Office Announcements & Customer Service Updates

**Purpose:** Maintain awareness and accountability

* Seasonal updates
* Customer service feedback
* Wins to repeat
* Mistakes to avoid

> *Operational clarity without overloading the morning.*

---

## ⬜ Morning Meeting Close (Motivational Send-Off)

**Purpose:** Inspire action and set emotional momentum for the day

* Delivered by an owner or senior leader
* 2–10 minutes
* Focus areas:

  * Belief
  * Energy
  * Gratitude
  * Purpose
* Pulls from:

  * Motivational Library

> *People don’t remember instructions — they remember how they felt walking out the door.*

---

## How to Use This Checklist

* Use daily to ensure nothing is skipped
* Rotate leaders through delivery roles
* Diagnose weak days by identifying missing sections
* Use as a training tool for new leaders

---

## Final Reminder

Consistency in the morning creates consistency in results.

**Strong mornings build strong days.**
