# Leadership Accountability & Ownership

## Core Story: Responsibility at the Highest Level

* **WWII – Dwight D. Eisenhower**

  * Took full responsibility for **D-Day, Normandy**
  * Accepted accountability for either **success or catastrophic failure**

> True leaders take responsibility **before** outcomes are known.

---

## Foundational Belief

* **Building a team helps build you**
* Leadership growth and team growth happen together

---

## Leadership Principles & Top Tips

### Expectations & Standards

* Set **clear expectations**
* Do **not** lower standards to create comfort
* Vague leadership creates weak results
* Inconsistency creates confusion
* **No excuses**:

  * Excuses allow weak work ethic
  * Excuses kill credibility fast

---

### Ownership & Adjustment

* Leaders don’t over-explain — **they adjust**
* Ownership creates solutions
* Accountability drives clarity

---

### Character Before Skill

* Install **good character** first
* Sales skills are teachable
* Character determines long-term success

---

### Daily Leadership Habits

* Daily check-ins matter
* Small conversations build big trust
* Early conversations prevent bigger problems later
* Leaders care enough to be **uncomfortable**

---

### Investment in People

* Invest in people through:

  * Time
  * Attention
  * Patience

* People perform better when they:

  * Feel believed in
  * Feel supported

---

### Observation & Awareness

* Point out both **what’s good and what needs improvement**
* People grow at the speed they feel invested in
* Observe **behaviors**, not just results
* Great leaders see issues **before** they become problems
* Observation builds trust because it shows you care

---

## Long-Term Perspective

* The habits you build determine:

  * Your role
  * Your influence
  * Your place in the business

---

## Leadership Reminder

Leadership is ownership.
Ownership is action.
Action builds trust.
