# 10 Commandments of Leadership

---

## 1. Uncompromising Commitment to Hitting Objectives

* People buy into **your goals** before setting their own
* Your clarity and conviction set the ceiling for the team

---

## 2. High Standards in Values, Morals, and Actions

* Live by strong habits:

  * **2/3 good habits**
  * **4/3 bad habits**
* Remember: **Culture eats strategy for breakfast**

---

## 3. Inspire and Instill Confidence, Motivation, and Drive

* In individuals
* In the team as a whole
* Confidence is contagious

---

## 4. Apply Personal Characteristics

* People are attracted to the **real version of you**
* Be the best version of yourself
* Authenticity builds trust

---

## 5. Uphold High Standards for Yourself

* Lead yourself before leading others
* Personal discipline creates organizational discipline

---

## 6. Be Adaptable and Flexible During Challenging Circumstances

* People learn more from **what you do** than what you say
* Your response to adversity becomes the lesson

---

## 7. Commitment to Never-Ending Improvement

* You can only teach people to get to **where you are at**
* Growth in the leader equals growth in the team

---

## 8. Projection of an Impeccable Image

* How you show up matters
* Consistency, professionalism, and presence build credibility

---

## 9. Enjoy Challenges and Have Fun

* Challenges are about **perception**
* The best leaders bring energy, optimism, and perspective

---

## 10. Strive for Commitment to Consistency

* What you have done matters less than **what you are doing**
* Consistent action compounds into long-term results

---
