# Save the Children — Charity Product Knowledge (Fundraise-Coach RAG)

1. **Official name / common name**

* Save the Children (Save the Children Federation / Save the Children USA)

2. **Founded / founder / origins**

* **Founded:** **1919**
* **Founder:** **Eglantyne Jebb**, British social reformer
* **Origins:** Founded in the aftermath of World War I to provide humanitarian aid to children suffering from war, famine, and poverty
* Eglantyne Jebb later helped draft the **Declaration of the Rights of the Child**, a foundational document for modern child rights

3. **Headquarters**

* **Global headquarters:** London, United Kingdom (Save the Children International)
* **Save the Children USA headquarters:** Fairfield, Connecticut, USA

4. **Mission statement**

* *Save the Children believes every child deserves a future.*
* The organization works to ensure children survive, learn, and are protected from harm.

5. **What they’re most known for**

* One of the **largest and most recognized children’s charities in the world**
* Emergency response in humanitarian crises (war, natural disasters, famine)
* Global leadership in child health, education, and protection
* Strong advocacy for children’s rights

6. **Expense breakdown (Save the Children USA – recent public reporting)**

* **Program services:** ~**85–87%**
* **Management & general (administration):** ~6–7%
* **Fundraising:** ~7–8%
* Consistently directs the majority of funds toward programmatic impact

7. **Charity ratings / watchdogs**

* **Charity Navigator:** **Four-Star Charity**, typically **97–99% overall rating**
* **BBB Wise Giving Alliance:** **Meets Standards for Charity Accountability**
* **CharityWatch:** **A-**

8. **Where they work (global reach)**

* Active in **110+ countries** worldwide
* Operates through Save the Children International and local country offices

9. **What they do in the United States**

* Disaster relief and recovery (hurricanes, wildfires, floods, winter storms)
* Early childhood education and literacy programs
* Hunger relief and food access
* Support for rural and underserved children

10. **Major programs / focus areas**

* **Health & nutrition** (vaccinations, maternal/child health, malnutrition treatment)
* **Education** (early learning, literacy, school access)
* **Child protection** (abuse prevention, safe spaces, child rights)
* **Humanitarian response & emergencies**
* **Poverty alleviation & family resilience**

11. **Programs / giving options**

* Emergency response funds
* Child protection and education funds
* Hunger and nutrition programs
* Monthly giving and general/unrestricted donations
* Corporate partnerships and planned giving

12. **Partnerships & affiliations**

* United Nations agencies (e.g., UNICEF partners, WFP collaborations)
* National and local governments
* Local NGOs and community-based organizations
* Corporate and institutional partners

13. **Impact & milestones (fundraiser talking points)**

* Reaches **tens of millions of children** annually through programs
* Active for **100+ years**, making it one of the oldest global children’s charities
* Responds rapidly to nearly every major humanitarian crisis affecting children worldwide
* Instrumental in advancing global child rights and protections

14. **CEO / leadership**

* **Save the Children USA President & CEO:** Janti Soeripto
* **Reported compensation:** Approximately **$500k–$600k annually** (varies by filing year)

15. **Public perception / context**

* Widely trusted, highly visible global charity
* Known for scale, speed of response, and long-term development programs
* Occasionally scrutinized due to size, but consistently rated highly by watchdogs

16. **Celebrity endorsements / affiliates**

* Historically associated with high-profile advocates (authors, actors, global ambassadors)
* Focus is on mission credibility and institutional partnerships rather than celebrity branding

17. **Websites & online presence**

* **Save the Children USA:** [https://www.savethechildren.org](https://www.savethechildren.org)
* **Save the Children International:** [https://www.savethechildren.net](https://www.savethechildren.net)
* Active on major social platforms (Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter, YouTube, LinkedIn)

18. **Fundraiser-ready summary (one-liner)**

* *Save the Children is a century-old global nonprofit working in over 110 countries to ensure children survive, learn, and are protected—responding to emergencies while building long-term solutions for the world’s most vulnerable kids.*
